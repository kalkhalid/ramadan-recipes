Lentil Soup

Lentil soup is a comforting soup!

Lentil soup is easy to make with only 4 simple ingredients and only takes 5 minutes of prep. The ideal last mintue healthy iftar dish.

Ingredients

    Red lentil onions Vegetables Water Spices:Cumin,7 spice, salt & pepper Fresh Parsley and Lemon juice

Recipe Instruction

  1. Rinse the lentils until the water runs clear.
  2. Dice the onions and prepare any vegetables you prefer.
  3. Saute onions then add the lentils and vegetables.
  4. Add hot water and cook the lentils and vegetables until softened (about 45 min).
  5. Blend your soup.
  6. Add your spices and let it simmer for an additional 15 min on lower heat .
  7. Garnish with parsley and lemon juice.
