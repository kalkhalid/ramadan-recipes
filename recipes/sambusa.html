Sambusa

Sambusas are the most popular traditional Somali food for Ramadan.

Sambusas, also known as samosas, are one of the most common dishes served during Ramadan in Somali households.

They are crispy, spicy, and an addictive savory pastry.

Ingredients

  • Vegetable oil
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 1/2 pound lean ground beef

  • 1 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper

Recipe Instruction

  • Beef Mixture
    1. heat 1 tablespoon oil over medium heat.
    2. Add the onion and cook about 4 minutes.
    3. Add the beef, parsley, curry powder, garlic, salt, and pepper, and cook until the meat is fully cooked.
  • Prepare Flour Paste
    1. In a small bowl combine flour with water until a watery paste is formed
